About Elise

coach · mentor · recovering overthinker

I built the "right" life first. Then I built mine.

Ten years in corporate strategy taught me how to hit targets — and how quietly miserable a well-decorated life can feel when the targets aren't yours. The morning I caught myself rehearsing enthusiasm in a bathroom mirror, I knew something had to change.

Retraining as a coach wasn't a leap of faith; it was a plan, built the same way I now build them with clients — one honest, doable step at a time. Twelve years and four hundred women later, that's still the whole method.
